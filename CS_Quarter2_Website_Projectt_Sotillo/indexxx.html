BLACK CLOVER

Set in a world where magic is everything, the story follows Asta, a young boy born without any magic power who is given a rare grimoire that grants him anti-magic abilities. With his fellow mages from the Black Bulls, Asta plans to become the next Wizard King!

Created by Yuki Tabata | Studio Pierrot

Read Black Clover Manga

Latest: Vol.32

Asta is a young boy who dreams of becoming the greatest mage in the kingdom. Only one problem he can't use any magic! Luckily for Asta, he receives the incredibly rare five-leaf clover grimoire that gives him the power of anti-magic. Can someone who can't use magic really become the Wizard King? One thing's for sure, Asta will never give up!

To save Yami, the Black Bulls storm their way into enemy territory. And with a powered-up Asta joining the fight, the battle may be going their way. But when the most powerful devil in the underworld finally makes his appearance, the entire world may be on the verge of destruction!

Publication Date: March 7, 2023
